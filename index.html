Problem Statements

Problem Statement 1: Flood Management using AI

Floods are among the most destructive natural disasters, causing loss of life, property damage, and long-term environmental impacts. Develop an AI-powered early warning system that predicts flood risks by analyzing weather data, river levels, and historical patterns to enable timely evacuations and resource deployment.

Problem Statement 2: Smart Farming using IoT and AI

Modern agriculture struggles with water scarcity, unpredictable weather, and declining soil health. Create an IoT-based smart farming solution that monitors soil moisture, temperature, and crop health in real-time, using AI to optimize irrigation, predict yields, and reduce resource waste.

Problem Statement 3: AI-IoT based Cardiac Health Monitoring

Traditional healthcare systems largely rely on periodic checkups, which may miss critical early warning signs of cardiac issues. Design an AI-IoT based wearable system that continuously monitors heart rate, blood pressure, and other vital signs, providing real-time alerts and predictive insights to prevent cardiac emergencies.

Problem Statement 4: AI-IoT based Traffic Management System

Urban areas worldwide are facing increasing traffic congestion, leading to wasted time, fuel consumption, and pollution. Develop an intelligent traffic management system using IoT sensors and AI algorithms to optimize traffic flow, reduce congestion, and improve emergency vehicle routing in smart cities.
